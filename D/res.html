🇮🇳 Prime Minister's Internship Scheme

Objective: To provide opportunities for Indian youth to gain practical experience in various government departments and ministries, fostering skills and an understanding of public service.

Key Guidelines:

  • Eligibility: Must be an Indian citizen. Minimum qualification: Graduation.
  • Duration: 2 to 6 months.
  • Stipend: A monthly stipend is provided to cover expenses.
  • Application: Applications are typically opened on the official portal twice a year.
